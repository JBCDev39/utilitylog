# UtilityLog — Setup Instructions

## How to get this on your phone as an app

### Step 1 — Create a free GitHub account
Go to https://github.com and sign up (free).

### Step 2 — Create a new repository
1. Click the **+** icon (top right) → **New repository**
2. Name it: `utilitylog`
3. Set it to **Public**
4. Check **"Add a README file"**
5. Click **Create repository**

### Step 3 — Upload the files
1. In your new repository, click **Add file** → **Upload files**
2. Upload ALL files from this folder:
   - index.html
   - app.js
   - manifest.json
   - sw.js
   - icons/ (the whole folder with both .png files)
3. Click **Commit changes**

### Step 4 — Enable GitHub Pages
1. Go to your repository **Settings**
2. Scroll to **Pages** (left sidebar)
3. Under **Source**, select **Deploy from a branch**
4. Branch: **main**, folder: **/ (root)**
5. Click **Save**
6. Wait ~2 minutes, then your app is live at:
   `https://YOUR-USERNAME.github.io/utilitylog`

### Step 5 — Install on your phone

**iPhone (Safari):**
1. Open the link above in Safari
2. Tap the **Share** button (box with arrow)
3. Tap **Add to Home Screen**
4. Tap **Add** — done!

**Android (Chrome):**
1. Open the link in Chrome
2. Tap the **three dots** menu
3. Tap **Add to Home screen**
4. Tap **Add** — done!

---

Your app icon will appear on your home screen.
All data is stored on your device — nothing goes to any server.
Works fully offline once installed.
